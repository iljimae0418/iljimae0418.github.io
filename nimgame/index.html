Modified Nim Game

Let's play the "Modified Nim Game". This game is played by two people, namely me and you.

There are N stones on the table. We take turns and remove the stone from the table. Only 1,3 or 4 stones can be removed at each turn.

The person who takes the last stone is the winner!
