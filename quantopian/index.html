  • EMA crossover algorithm

    The algorithm calculates the exponential moving average (EMA) of the apple stock for a 17 day window, and also calculates the exponential moving average of the apple stock for a 40 day window. If the 17 day window EMA is greater than 40 day EMA we place order of 40% of our funds and if the 40 day window EMA is greater, we sell.

    The backtest results were interesting. Used data from 2011-01-04 to 2018-02-16 with 10,000,000 initial funds and total return was 1059% which was better than the benchmark performance of 146%. However sharpe ratio is less than 1 but in fact most traders desire a sharpe ratio greater than 3.
