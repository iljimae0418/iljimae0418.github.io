Note that \(x \neq 0 \), so we may divide the equation by \(x^2\) to obtain \(x^2+ax+2+\frac{b}{x}+\frac{1}{x^2} \). This may be simplified as the following $$ (x+\frac{a}{2})^2 + (\frac{1}{x}+\frac{b}{2})^2 = \frac{a^2+b^2}{4}-2$$. Because the left hand side of the equation is greater than or equal to 0, \(\frac{a^2+b^2}{4}-2 \geq 0 \), and we get $$a^2+b^2 \geq 8$$ which makes the minimum value of \(a^2 + b^2 \) equal to 8.
