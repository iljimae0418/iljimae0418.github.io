Note that $$2\sqrt{13}-x^2+k^2-13 = k^2-(x-\sqrt{13})^2 $$ $$ \sqrt{k^2-(x-\sqrt{13})^2} \leq k \therefore \frac{1}{1004}\sum_{k=1}^{2008}\sqrt{k^2-(x-\sqrt{13})^2} \leq \frac{1}{1004}\sum_{k=1}^{2008}k = 2009$$. For equality to hold, $$ \sqrt{k^2-(x-\sqrt{13})^2} = k \therefore x = p = \sqrt{13}$$, so \(p^4 = 169 \).
