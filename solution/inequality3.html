From AM-GM inequality, we have $$ \frac{a^8 + x^4 + x^4 + x^4}{4} \geq (a^8x^{12})^{\frac{1}{4}} = a^2x^3 ... (1) $$ $$ \frac{b^8 + y^4 + y^4 + y^4}{4} \geq (b^8y^{12})^{\frac{1}{4}} = b^2y^3 ... (2) $$ \((1)+(2)\) yields $$ 1 \geq a^2x^3 + b^2y^3 $$ so the maxvalue \(k\) is 1 and \(2017k = 2017 \).
