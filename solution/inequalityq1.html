Area of \(\bigtriangleup ABC = \frac{1}{2} \times \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} \times 4 \times 4 = 4\sqrt{3}\) \(\therefore A_1 + A_2 + A_3 = 4\sqrt{3}\). From Cauchy-Schwarz Inequality , $$ (A_1^2 + A_2^2 + A_3^3)(1^2 + 1^2 + 1^2) \geq (A_1 + A_2 + A_3)^2 \therefore A_1^2 + A_2^2 + A_3^3 \geq \frac{1}{3}(4\sqrt{3})^2 = 16 $$ so the minimum value is 16.
